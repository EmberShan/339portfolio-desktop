Impressionism

An art movement led by Claude Monet and other Paris-based artists. It features small, thin, yet visible brush strokes.

It starts first in painting then in music.

Neo-Impressionism

Neo-impressionism utilizes Divisionism which means using dots of different colors to represent an object instead of mixing the colors together.

Post-Impressionism

Instead of optical impressions, Post-impressionism expresses emotions on themes of deeper symbolism.
